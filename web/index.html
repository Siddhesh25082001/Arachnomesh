    About the Channelier Fmcg Awards

    Channelier FMCG Awards acknowledge excellence in areas such as brand strength, product launch, innovation in advertising and marketing across FMCG sectors. We are partners with India’s leading online and offline FMCG retailers and marketplaces. Channelier FMCG Awards is set to be the most happening event of the year for the industry. These awards present a unique opportunity for FMCG brands to get recognised and showcase themselves and their products to the Indian market.

    Who can participate?

    Any and every Indian FMCG brand can participate in the event. The competition has 3 awards categories across product groups to choose from and is ideal for both big and small companies. Each company can register their nomination in multiple categories.
